How It Works

Get started with WearLoop in four simple steps. Rent, share, and style sustainably.

01
Sign Up & Upload Selfie
Create your account and upload a selfie for AI skin analysis. Get personalized color recommendations instantly.
02
Browse or Upload
Explore the marketplace for outfits or upload your own clothes to rent out and start earning.
03
Get AI Recommendations
Chat with our AI stylist or browse AI-recommended items that match your skin tone and style preferences.
04
Rent & Enjoy
Select your perfect outfit, complete the rental, and enjoy wearing sustainable fashion at affordable prices.
